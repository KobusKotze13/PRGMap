PRG Sentrum | Centre

Die Paul Roos Sentrum is gebou toe die Japie Krige-saal te klein geword het vir die groot leerdergetalle.
Op 20 Maart 2002 is die gebou amptelik deur oudpresident Nelson Mandela geopen.

The Paul Roos Centre was commissioned when the Japie Krige Hall became too small to seat the growing number of learners at the school.
On the 20 March 2002, the building was declared open by former president, Nelson Mandela.
