Johan J Ferreira Hoë-Prestasiesentrum | High Performance Centre


Die Johan J Ferreira Hoëprestasiesentrum (2023) is die grootste en mees gevorderde fasiliteit van sy soort in die land.
Dié sentrum spog met die allernuutste toerusting en ’n voltydse span kundiges wat sorg dat alle leerders se liggaamlike ontwikkeling, ongeag hul vermoëns, bevorder word.
Hierdie geriewe bied ongeëwenaarde geleenthede vir groei en prestasies.

The Johan J Ferreira High Performance Centre (2023) stands as the largest and most advanced facility of its kind in the country.
Complete with state-of-the-art equipment and a dedicated team of full-time professionals, every learner undergoes a comprehensive journey of physical development, regardless of their abilities.
This centre serves as the pinnacle of high performance, offering unparalleled opportunities for growth and achievement.
