Musiekblok | Music Block


In die musiekblok word die klanke van kreatiwiteit en blydskap gehoor! Hiér kry leerders die geleentheid om hul passie vir musiek te ontdek. Van kitaarmelodieë tot koorsang, elke vertrek bied ’n unieke omgewing waarbinne leerders, met die hulp van toegewyde kundiges, hul musiektalent kan ontwikkel.

The music block is a lively space filled with the sounds of creativity and joy, where students explore their passion for music through various classes. From guitar melodies to choral arrangements, each room offers a unique environment for students to develop their musical talents with guidance from dedicated instructors.
