Akwatiese Sentrum | Aquatic Centre


Die Anthonij Rupert Akwatiese Sentrum spog met ’n waterpoloswembad van olimpiese grootte en ’n asemrowende uitsig op die omliggende berge. Dit is die kern van alle watersportaktiwiteite.

The Anthonij Rupert Aquatic Centre boasts an Olympic-sized waterpolo pool, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. It serves as the epicentre for aquatic activities, providing a focal point for all water-related endeavours.
