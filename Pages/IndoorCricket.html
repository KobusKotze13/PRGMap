Binnehuise krieketsentrum | Indoor Cricket Centre


Dit sal moeilik wees om ’n meer moderne en meer tegnologies gevorderde binnehuise krieketsentrum in Suid-Afrika te kry! Dié krieketsentrum spog met ses nette, verskillende oppervlakke, die allernuutste tegnologie wat regdeur die jaar oefengeleenthede vir boulers en kolwers bied.

In South Africa, finding a more modern and technologically advanced indoor cricket center would be a challenge. Equipped with six nets, diverse surfaces, and cutting-edge technology, our facility offers unparalleled training opportunities for bowlers and batters throughout the year.
