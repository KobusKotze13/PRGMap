Markötter Sportkompleks | Sporting Complex


Die bekende Markötter Sportkompleks met sy asemrowende berguitsigte is ’n baken in Stellebosch. Hier is vyf rugbyvelde, ’n hoofpawiljoen met plek vir 3000 toeskouers, binnehuise geriewe met ’n halwe veld en die indrukwekkende drieverdiepingklubhuis.

The renowned Markötter Sporting Complex, offering breathtaking views of the mountains, holds a prominent position in Stellenbosch. It features five expansive rugby fields, a grandstand which accommodates 3000 spectators overlooking the main field, a half-field indoor facility and the distinguished three-story clubhouse.
