Roosinbosch


Roosinbosch is PRG se kafeteria, snoepie en koffiewinkel. Hier kan jy lekker ontspan, huiswerk doen, jou selfoon laai en selfs die Oude Leeskamer vir groeptake bespreek.

Roosinbosch serves as the cafeteria, tuck shop and coffee shop at Paul Roos. Here, you can unwind, complete your homework, charge your cellphone and even reserve the Oude Leeskamer for group assignments.
