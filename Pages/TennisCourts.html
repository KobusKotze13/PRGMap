Tennisbane | Tennis Courts


Die At Slabber-tennisbane sluit ses bane in. Twee van die bane het spreiligte wat verseker dat ontluikende tennisspelers vroegoggend en laataand kan oefen.

The At Slabber tennis courts consist of six courts. Two of the courts are equipped with flood lights so that budding tennis players can practise early in the morning and later into the evening.
