Mediasentrum | Media Centre


Die mediasentrum bied akademiese ondersteuning aan PRG-leerders. In hierdie intellektueel stimulerende omgewing kan hulle lees, huiswerk voltooi en navorsing doen. Dié sentrum, wat sentraal geleë is, bevorder ’n leeskultuur en die versameling boeke word aangevul gebaseer op die belangstellings van die PRG-leerders. Rekenaars en ’n virtuele realiteit-hokkie verseker dat leerders toegang tot ’n verskeidenheid hulpbronne het wat hul leerervaring kan ondersteun.

The Media Centre offers academic support to all PRG learners. Here they can do homework and project research or read in an intellectually stimulated environment. The Centre promotes and develops a culture of reading and the book collection is built on the reading interests of PRG learners. It is strategically positioned in the heart of the school grounds. Evolving with modern trends, the Centre offers access to computers and features a virtual reality booth, ensuring that students have access to a diverse range of resources to complement their learning experience.
