Markötter Klubhuis | Clubhouse


Die Markötter Klubhuis beslaan drie verdiepings en sluit die “Long Room”, die “Suite” en die uitkykdek in. In die “Long Room” word funksies aangebied. In die “Suite” word afrigters en BBP’s onthaal terwyl hulle spannende rugbywedstryde kyk. Die uitkykdek word vir groot derby-wedstryde gereserveer.

The Markötter clubhouse, spanning three stories, comprises the Long Room, the Suite and the viewing deck. The Long Room serves as a venue for functions. The Suite, enveloped in historical ambiance, hosts coaches and VIPs who spectate thrilling matches on the main field and the public viewing deck is reserved for major derby days.
