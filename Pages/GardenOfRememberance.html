Tuin van Herinnering | Garden of Remembrance


In die vreedsame Tuin van Herinnering word ons gevalle broers herdenk en vereer. Gedenkplate met die name van oudleerders wat tydens die Anglo-Boereoorlog, die twee wêreldoorloë, die Grensoorloë en die Vryheidstryd gesterf het, kan in hierdie tuin besigtig word.

The Garden of Remembrance is a quiet memorial where we pay tribute to and honour our fallen brothers. Plaques bearing the names of all Old Boys who bravely gave their lives during the Anglo-Boer War, the World Wars, the Border War and the Freedom Struggle adorn the pathways, flanking the tranquil fountains.
