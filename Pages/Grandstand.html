Hoofpawiljoen | Grandstand


Die hoofpawiljoen is die hart van PRG-gees, die “Roos” en die formidabele Maroen Masjien. Dit is hier waar die geesdriftige dreunsang van die 1300 Paul Roosers op Saterdae weerklink wanneer die eerste span op die veld draf.

This is the epicentre of the "Gees", the "Roos" and the formidable Maroon Machine, where the spirited cheers of Paul Roosers reverberate throughout the neighbourhood as the 1300-brotherhood passionately rally behind the first XV on a crisp, winter Saturday, giving it their all.
