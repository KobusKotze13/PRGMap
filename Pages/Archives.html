Die Paul Roos Gimnasium-argief bied ’n fassinerende blik op die verlede. ’n Mens kan ou foto’s, boeke, briewe en ander artifakte bestudeer wat lig werp op die skool sy ryke geskiedenis. Hiér kan jy meer inligting bekom oor die invloedryke figure wat in die gange van Paul Roos Gimnasium gestap het.

The Paul Roos Gymnasium archives offer a fascinating glimpse into the past. You can explore old photographs, books, letters and other artifacts that shed light on the school's rich heritage. It's a great opportunity to learn about the influential figures who have walked the halls of Paul Roos and the contributions they have made to South African society.
