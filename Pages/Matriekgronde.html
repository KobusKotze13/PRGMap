Matriekgronde | Matric Grounds


Die matriekgronde is ’n toevlugsoord vir die graad 12-leerders. Dit is ’n plek waar blywende vriendskappe gesmee en onvergeetlike herinneringe gemaak word. Om die matriekgronde te betree, is ’n merkwaardige deel van elke Paul Rooser se skoolreis.

The matric grounds serve as a cherished sanctuary for the school's Grade 12 learners, zealously guarded by each year's matriculating class. It's a place where enduring friendships are forged, and unforgettable memories are created, marking a significant chapter in every Paul Rooser's journey.
