Oudskoliere Kantoor | Old Boys' Office


Die Oudskoliere Kantoor word deur die vrygewige bydraes van oudleerders onderhou om te verseker dat verhoudings en konneksies wêreldwyd en oor generasies heen met die alumni bevorder word. Dié kantoor is verantwoordelik vir reünies, fondsinsamelings en ondersteuning van verskeie skoolaktiwiteite.

The Old Boys' Office is sustained through the generous contributions of former students, maintaining connections and relationships with Alumni spanning generations worldwide. In addition to organising reunions, they actively fundraise and provide support for various school activities.
